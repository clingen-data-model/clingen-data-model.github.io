AggregateCosegregationStatement

  1. Interpretation Model
  2. Entities

A statement describing the observed correlation between transmission of a particular allele and a particular genetic condition, across multiple family pedigrees (i.e. the co-segregation of an allele and a condition as observed across multiple family pedigrees).

SEPIO:0000248
Descended from Statement
