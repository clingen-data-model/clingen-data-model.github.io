MolecularConsequence

  1. Interpretation Model
  2. Technical Reference
  3. Entity Definitions
  4. Domain Entities

A classification of a genomic variant based on its effect on a transcript or its location with respect to the transcript.

SEPIO-CG:55102
Descended from DomainEntity

Scope and Usage

The values of MolecularConsequence denote the downstream effects of an allele on transcriptional or translational entities produced by a gene. When an allele is not located in a translated region, the MolecularConsequence is defined by the type of region in which it lies, such as intron or UTR.

This DomainEntity wraps values from a ValueSet, see the discussion for more on this topic.
