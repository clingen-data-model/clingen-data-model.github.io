Disease

From MONDO: An alteration of health status resulting from a physiopathological mechanism, and having a homogeneous clinical presentation and evolution and homogeneous therapeutic possibilities.

SEPIO-CG:55116
Descended from DomainEntity

Scope and Usage

The values of Disease correspond to diseases as defined in one of several IdentifierSystems including MeSH, Disease Ontology, OMIM, and MONDO.

This DomainEntity wraps values from a ValueSet, see the discussion for more on this topic. In particular this entity is not fully instantiated in the interpretation model, but is defined in terms of the identifiers produced by external IdentifierSystems.
