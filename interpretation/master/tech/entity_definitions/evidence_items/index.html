Scope and Usage

Interpretations are created by reasoning over Statements. Individual atomic pieces of evidence are represented by Statement elements. Statement has numerous subclasses, tailored to represent the types of evidence used in the evaluation of ACMG Variant Pathogenicity Guidelines; expansion of the interpretation model into other arenas will also expand the set of available Statement types.

The Statement entity itself provides no attributes besides those inherited from Entity. Instances of Statement should be instances of a subclass providing data-specific attributes.
