A statement describing the presence of a particular disease appears in the history of a family.

SEPIO:0000239
Descended from Statement

Scope and Usage

Previous incidence of a condition in a family provides important clues as to the mechanism. For instance, if a family has multiple instances of a condition, then a DeNovo allele is an unlikely mechanism. This data element associates a family with a condition and states whether one or more family members, other than the proband, have the condition.

Attributes

Name Type Cardinality Description IRI
proband Individual 0..1 The person that was the original source of the condition in this family SEPIO:0000281
condition GeneticCondition 0..* A condition that the family may or may not have SEPIO:0000276
familyHasCondition ConditionStatus 1..1 Whether or not this family has the stated condition SEPIO:0000197
